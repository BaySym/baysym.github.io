Poetry Machine

About

My role on this project was to develop a use case of AI-generated poetry.

I worked alone, with weekly supervision meetings, using Figma for digital mock-ups.

The project was completed over a span of six weeks between July and August 2020.

Brief

Due to the popularity of artificial intelligence's usage and its ever-increasing capabilities, it is not unreasonable to image it producing bespoke media such as literature, TV shows, and feature films, in the near future.

This project's brief was to conceptualise and design such a bespoke-media system which would generate poetry.
