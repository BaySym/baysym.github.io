CMP3754 Assessment 1

Virtual and Augmented Reality assessment brief,

Virtual Reality

Complete workshops 1-6, then write a report which presents an analysis of your street scene, using the concepts and theory presented in your lectures. The report should contain the following sections, each around 500 words and fully referenced to back up your arguments:

Graphic fidelity:

This section should discuss the graphical fidelity of the scene, and how it might affect users’ sense of presence. You should consider positive and negative points and consider how fidelity can be optimised.



Locomotion:

This section should discuss 3 different locomotion methods, their appropriateness for the simulation, and each might affect users’ sense of presence.



Agent behaviour:

This section should discuss agent behaviour and how it might affect users’ experience (including, but not limited to, sense of presence), what improvements could be made, and how they would improve user experience?



VR sickness:

This section should discuss factors that might affect users experience of VR sickness in the simulation, and what improvements could be made to help users overcome VR sickness.



References unlimited length

You may vary the amount you write for each section, but the report must not exceed a maximum of 2000 words excluding references (presentation penalties may be applied if the maximum word count is exceeded).

Augmented Reality

Researchers are already attempting to find new and beneficial ways to use AR in education and training situations. The military and healthcare industries, in particular, are developing powerful AR training simulations.

Your task is to develop an educational/informative Augmented Reality mobile app which utilises the AR recognition and tracking capabilities provided in workshops, and your own independent study. This must be accompanied by the development and integration of a GUI (Graphical User Interface) which disseminates additional content/information.

You may choose the specific educational theme, but your AR experience must:

1. Combine virtual and real information, with the real world as the primary place of action.


2. Be interactive with real-time updates to content or object (GUI).


3. Have virtual information registered in 3D space, in the physical environment.

You must also write a report consisting of the following sections, supported by relevant methodologies and academic literature where appropriate:

Overview max 500 words:

This section should discuss the graphical fidelity of the scene, and how it might affect users’ sense of presence. You should consider positive and negative points and consider how fidelity can be optimised.



Development max 750 words:

Discuss your development including any technical implementation(s) for example handling Interruptions or using audio and haptics to enhance the immersive experience. This should include a rationale behind your choice of implementation and the intended user experience.



Analysis and evaluation max 750 words:

This could include a discussion on environment, interactions and patterns, cognitive tunneling, diegetic/ non diegetic interfaces and/or other aspects that you think are relevant. You should also discuss any negative aspects.



References unlimited length

Each section must not exceed the maximum word count indicated.
