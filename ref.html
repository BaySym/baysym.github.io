Frequently used citation styles from the University of Lincoln's referencing guide book.


books
Author (Year) Title. Place of Publication: Publisher.


journal articles
Author (Year) Article title. Journal title, volume and issue number, page range.


Lectures and seminars: Lecturer (Year) Title. [lecture/seminar]. Module title and code, education institution, date of lecture/seminar, available from [date of access].


Theses and dissertations: Author (Year) Title. Qualification level. Education institution. Available from [date of access]


Websites: Professional body (Year) Title. Place of publication: Publisher. Available from [date of access]
